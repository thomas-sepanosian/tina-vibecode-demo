During my research I explored how inertial measurement units (IMUs) can be used to recognize boxing gestures in real time. The work resulted in two published papers and a live dashboard integration with the OpenEarable platform.

## Publications

- **Training Smarter with OpenEarable: A Boxing Gesture Recognition Dashboard Integration**
  Sepanosian and Incel — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1145/3675094.3678481)

- **Boxing Gesture Recognition in Real-Time using Wearable IMUs**
  Sepanosian and Incel — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1145/3675094.3680524)
